# Design

## Visual Direction

Secure Smart should feel like a working B2B distributor catalogue: light, structured, quiet, credible and product-led. The UI should be closer to EETGroup-style procurement than a marketing landing page. Avoid dramatic gradients, giant display typography and decorative effects.

## Color

Use a restrained palette.

- Content surface: tinted white / very light blue-gray.
- Page background: cool off-white.
- Text: deep blue-gray, never pure black.
- Muted text: medium slate, readable on white.
- Primary accent: professional blue used only for primary actions, active states and key links.
- Success accent: reserved for availability/approval indicators.
- Huawei appears as text only, no logo tile styling beyond neutral brand label.

## Typography

Use Assistant for Hebrew and Inter/system sans for English/SKUs. Keep the scale modest.

- Hero title: 36-44px desktop, about 28-30px mobile.
- Section titles: 26-34px desktop.
- Body: 15-17px.
- UI labels/product metadata: 11-14px.
- Avoid display fonts and theatrical sizing.

## Layout

The page is a product-style B2B portal.

- Utility bar: short commercial trust signals.
- Header: logo, search, quote/catalogue/account actions.
- Navigation: About, Categories, Brands, Products, Trade account.
- Hero: concise positioning plus a functional product preview, not a decorative hero image.
- Brand strip: compact, grouped brand labels.
- About: only essential positioning, no filler.
- Categories: compact entry points into the catalogue.
- Catalogue: filter sidebar plus product grid.
- Trade account: RFQ/account request form.
- Footer: brands, service, Huawei limitation note.

Spacing should use a small consistent product-UI scale. Related items stay tight; sections have enough separation without feeling airy or empty.

## Components

- Buttons: one vocabulary, small radius, restrained blue primary, white secondary.
- Search: prominent and functional.
- Product cards: image, brand, name, SKU, specs, locked Trade price state, RFQ/details actions.
- Filters: familiar checkbox sidebar, no custom novelty.
- Forms: standard inputs and selects, clear labels/placeholders.

## Motion

Use only subtle hover/focus transitions around 150-180ms. No page-load choreography, no decorative animation.

## Responsive

Mobile should remain a website, not a poster. Header/search may stack, nav can hide, product grid becomes one column, filter sidebar stacks above products. Avoid sticky CTAs that cover content.
